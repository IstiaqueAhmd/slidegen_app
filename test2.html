Kitten Care Essentials

  1. 1

    Nutrition Protocol

    Feed kitten-formulated food rich in protein (>30%), calcium (1%), and DHA omega-3 until 12 months

  2. 2

    Environmental Enrichment

    Provide 3 vertical spaces, rotated bi-weekly toys, and puzzle feeders to stimulate development

    Preventative Healthcare

    Vaccinate at 8/12/16 weeks, spay/neuter by 5 months, and schedule monthly weight checks

    Positive Bonding
